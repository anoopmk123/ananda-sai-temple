About The Temple

Founded by N.V. Ramanan, the story began in 1997 when a palm reader revealed he would build a temple. In 1998, Sai Baba appeared in his dream saying "I will come in a new form ‚Äî construct a temple for me."

Through divine grace, Mr. Leo Muthu donated 33 acres of land. Dubai-based devotee Mrs. Surya was guided in a dream to Poonthandalam, where she commissioned Asia's unique Sai pratima.
